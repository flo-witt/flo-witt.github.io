I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Computer Science at University of Duisburg-Essen, working on approximative fixpoint theory with applications to reinforcement learning under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Barbara König.

My broader research interests include formal guarantees for (deep) reinforcement learning agents, and the quantitative verification and control of probabilistic and of concurrent systems.
